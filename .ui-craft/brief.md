# K문서툴 Design Brief

## Product Identity
K문서툴 is a Korean utility site for PDF, image, and business document tasks. The product should feel fast, practical, and trustworthy rather than decorative or template-like.

## Audience
People who need to prepare submission files quickly: freelancers, small business owners, office workers, and users converting phone photos or PDFs for Korean forms and portals.

## Design Intent
- Search-first utility surface.
- Minimal clean product UI.
- Dense enough to scan many tools, but with clear spacing between cards.
- Use Korean copy sparingly. The tool itself should be the first thing users can act on.

## Signature Detail
A compact command surface with category-consistent icon color. Cards feel like a clean tool shelf, not a marketing feature grid.

## Learned Constraints
- Avoid AI-slop template patterns.
- Remove decorative gradients and ornamental background effects.
- Keep card groups visually separated like iTool: clear gaps, simple borders, no crowded ledger rows.
- Emphasize "설치 없음", "무료", and "서버 전송 없음".
- Same category tools should share the same color.
